About Me

Hi, I’m Kyle Lunsford — a Computer Science student with a concentration in Data Science at UNC Charlotte. I’m passionate about programming, web development, and using data to solve real-world problems. This website is both a portfolio of my class projects and a space to document my growth as a developer.

My interests span across multiple areas of technology, from building clean and functional web applications to analyzing data for meaningful insights. I enjoy the process of taking an idea, breaking it down into smaller steps, and bringing it to life through code. Along the way, I’ve worked with tools like Python, Java, JavaScript, and SQL, as well as libraries and frameworks for data visualization and web design.

Beyond academics, I enjoy staying active through sports, fitness, and outdoor activities. I’ve also worked as an intramural referee and a Land Guide at the Whitewater Center, which has helped me develop teamwork, communication, and leadership skills.

I’m always curious and eager to learn something new — whether that’s exploring a new programming language, diving deeper into data science, or experimenting with design ideas to improve my projects. My ultimate goal is to build skills that not only advance my career in tech but also create practical, impactful solutions for others.

Thanks for visiting my site! Feel free to browse through my projects and get a sense of how I approach problems, experiment with solutions, and continue to grow as a developer.
